**Location:** Denver, Colorado (Front Range).

## Zone and frost

- **USDA hardiness zone:** Metro Denver is often cited around **6a–6b** on current maps, with cooler pockets (e.g. higher elevation nearby) toward **5b**. Confirm for your exact site (microclimate, elevation).  
- **Frost (planning ballpark):** Local guides for Denver commonly use **average last spring frost around mid-May** (often described as near Mother’s Day) and **first fall frost in mid-October**, with large year-to-year variation. Use forecasts and your own yard’s frost history for planting decisions.  
- **Sources to keep handy:** [CSU Extension — Vegetable planting guide](https://extension.colostate.edu/resource/vegetable-planting-guide/); [City of Denver — When to plant what](https://denvergov.org/Community/Support/Food/Growing/Planting-Guide).  

These dates are for scheduling (`SCHEDULE.md`) and row-cover timing; adjust if your yard runs colder or warmer than “typical” Denver.

## Infrastructure

- Trellising / fence use in front yard for cucumbers, morning glory, spaghetti squash.  
- Row cover: prioritize box 4 (late March–mid-May), then box 3 (April–mid-May); optional early spring on box 2.  
- **Drip irrigation (draft zones):** timers/circuits and tubing routing to be shown on `layout/beds.svg` (per [CONVENTIONS.md](../CONVENTIONS.md)). Compost bin is on the same drip system; note which zone/timer feeds it when you diagram it.

### Drip — Zone 1 (greenhouse)

- **Plants:** Cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, jalapeños, sweet potatoes.  
- **Emitters:** 1–2 GPH per plant.  
- **Frequency:** Daily or near-daily at peak summer.  

### Drip — Zone 2 (all four raised boxes)

- **Plants:** Yellow onions, green onions, Brussels sprouts, outdoor oregano (boxes 1–2); kale, lemon balm (box 3); strawberries (box 4).  
- **Emitters:** **1 GPH** for most plants; **½ GPH** for onions and lemon balm. Partial-sun boxes 3–4 dry more slowly but share this zone — use **somewhat lower GPH in boxes 3–4** than in full-sun boxes 1–2 to compensate.  
- **Frequency:** Every 2–3 days.  

### Drip — Zone 3 (back yard — peach + cherry)

- **Plants:** Peach tree, cherry shrub.  
- **Emitters:** **2 GPH**, placed to cover a **wider root zone**.  
- **Frequency:** Every **4–5 days**, with **longer run times** for deep, infrequent watering (young trees/shrub, root development).  

**Front yard** vining crops and **indoor herbs** are not listed on these drip zones yet — hand water or add a future zone when decided.
